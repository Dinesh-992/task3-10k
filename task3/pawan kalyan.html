Preface

Pawan Kalyan, whose birth name is Konidala Kalyan Babu, is an Indian politician and actor. He has been serving as the 10th Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh since June 12, 2024, and also holds the portfolios of Minister of Panchayat Raj, Rural Development & Rural Water Supply; Environment, Forests, Science & Technology in the Government of Andhra Pradesh

Acting Career

Pawan Kalyan made his acting debut in the 1996 Telugu film "Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi" and has since appeared in numerous successful films. Some of his notable movies include "Gokulamlo Seeta" (1997), "Tholi Prema" (1998), "Thammudu" (1999), "Gabbar Singh" (2012), and "Attarintiki Daredi" (2013)

He has received accolades for his performances, including the Filmfare Award for Best Actor in Telugu for his role in "Gabbar Singh." Kalyan is known for his unique acting style and mannerisms, which have garnered him a large fan base and a cult following

Personal Life

Pawan Kalyan has been married three times. He met his third wife, Anna Lezhneva, a Russian citizen, during the shoot of the film "Teen Maar" in 2011. They got married in September 2013 and have a daughter named Polena Anjana Pawanova and a son named Mark Shankar Pawanovich

Political Career

Apart from his successful acting career, Pawan Kalyan is also actively involved in politics. He is the founder of the Janasena Party and has made significant contributions to Indian political history. In the 2024 Lok Sabha elections and Andhra Pradesh assembly elections, his party secured all the seats it contested, marking a historic victory

On June 12, 2024, Pawan Kalyan took charge as the Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh and assumed the responsibilities of the Minister of Panchayat Raj, Rural Development & Rural Water Supply; Environment, Forests, Science & Technology in the Government of Andhra Pradesh

Conclusion

Pawan Kalyan is a prominent figure in the Telugu film industry and Indian politics. He has achieved success as an actor and has made a significant impact with his political party. With his unique style and influential persona, he continues to be a popular figure among his fans and followers.
